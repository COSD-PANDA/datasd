Welcome to DataSD, the new home of the City of San Diego's Open Data Program

We have big plans for DataSD - the new home of the Open Data Program in San Diego. We want it to be a place for you to learn about government data, and learn how to get involved in the community. As we continue building the site, you will see a lot more on here, but meanwhile - and most importantly, make sure to review and vote on the new Open Data Inventory!
